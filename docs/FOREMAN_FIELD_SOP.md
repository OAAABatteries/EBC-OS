# EBC Field SOP — Foreman Daily Operations

**One page. Five rules. No excuses.**

---

## 1. Plans Live in the App

- Open the app > tap your project > **Drawings** tab
- Every drawing shows a **revision label** (e.g., "IFC Rev 3") and a **CURRENT** badge
- If you see **"UPDATE AVAILABLE"** — tap **Re-download** before you build anything
- If a drawing says **SUPERSEDED** — stop using it, the PM uploaded a newer version
- **Save drawings for offline** the night before if your jobsite has bad signal

> **Rule: If you can't confirm the revision is CURRENT, don't build from it. Call the PM.**

---

## 2. Update the Job Stage Daily

- Dashboard > tap the **phase tracker** for your project
- Move the stage forward when work actually starts on that phase
- This is how the office knows where you are — if you don't update it, they'll assume nothing changed

> **Rule: Update the stage before you leave the jobsite each day.**

---

## 3. Material Requests Go Through the App

- **Materials** tab > **+ Request**
- Include: what you need, how much, when you need it, and urgency level
- The PM approves it, then either a driver delivers or you pick it up
- You'll see the status change: Requested > Approved > Delivered

> **Rule: No phone calls for materials. If it's not in the app, it didn't happen.**

---

## 4. Clock In With a Photo

- **Clock In** button on your home screen
- The app will ask for your location (GPS) and a photo
- Take the photo — it's how we verify crew presence without arguments later
- Clock out the same way at the end of the day

> **Rule: Clock in when you arrive, clock out when you leave. Photo every time.**

---

## 5. Report Problems Immediately

- See something wrong? Tap the **flag icon** or go to **Report Problem**
- Describe it, tag the project, and submit
- The PM sees it in their action queue — no waiting for a phone call back

> **Rule: If something looks wrong, report it now. Don't wait for it to become expensive.**

---

## Quick Reference

| I need to...              | Where in the app            |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------|
| See current plans         | Project > Drawings          |
| Check my crew schedule    | Dashboard > Crew            |
| Request materials         | Materials > + Request       |
| Clock in/out              | Home screen button          |
| File a daily report       | Reports > + New Report      |
| Report a problem          | Flag icon / Report Problem  |
| Complete a JSA            | Safety > + New JSA          |

---

## For New Crew Members

1. Download the app (or open the web link on your phone)
2. Log in with the credentials the PM gave you
3. You'll see your schedule and clock-in button
4. That's it — clock in, do your work, clock out
